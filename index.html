SMS-Management-System-V-1.0-Beta

This SMS Management System is an multipurpose way to create an SMS gateway to handle various SMS APIs from Twilio , Send Grid ,** Plivo** etc . Create SMS Packages , SMS availability , Bulk SMS , Pricing *, *Rest APIs and many more features are included on it . Payment gateways like BrainTree, Paypal *, *Worldline *, *Stripe *, *Authorize.Net and 2Checkout are integrated .
